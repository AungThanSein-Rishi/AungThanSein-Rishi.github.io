Strategic Coffee Sales Report with Visualization

Project Overview

This interactive Coffee Sales Dashboard offers a real-time, data-driven overview of coffee sales performance across regions, timeframes, customer segments, and product types. Developed in Excel using advanced charting techniques and structured datasets, it enables sales leaders to monitor trends, evaluate customer loyalty, and optimize product strategies with clarity and precision.

Key Features

  • Monthly Sales & Profit Tracking:Bar and line charts display monthly sales and profits over time, with a 3-period moving average to highlight seasonal trends and performance stability.
  • Year-over-Year Performance: A comparative annual chart illustrates shifts in sales and profit shares from 2019 to 2022, showing peak performance in 2021 (31%).
  • Loyalty Card Impact: Loyalty members account for 53.65% of sales and 52.10% of total customers, reflecting positive ROI on loyalty engagement.
  • Coffee Type Breakdown: Stacked bars compare Arabica, Excelsa, Liberica, and Robusta by roast level (Dark, Light, Medium), showing Arabica leads in total quantity, with a strong preference for Dark roast (38%).

Technical Implementation

  • Excel Dashboarding: Utilizes dynamic visuals (bar, line, doughnut, and stacked bar charts) with slicers for year and month filters.
  • Structured Data Tables: Integrated from sheets like orders_details, products_details, and custs_details, cleaned and summarized in analytical views.
  • DAX & Calculations: While not built in Power BI, the dashboard simulates KPI logic using Excel formulas to track trends, averages, and group-level contributions.

Business Impact

  • Performance Monitoring: Enables monthly and yearly tracking of both revenue and profit, helping identify high and low-performing periods for promotional planning.
  • Customer-Centric Strategy: Insight into top spenders and loyalty card participation guides retention and upselling efforts.
  • Market Focus: Data highlights the dominance of the market, allowing for strategic expansion or tailored campaigns in underperforming regions.
