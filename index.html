About Me

Hi, I’m Aung Than Sein, also known as Rishi — a results-driven professional with over a decade of experience in Sales, Marketing, and Strategic Planning across the FMCG, Telecom & Insurance industries. I specialize in data-driven decision-making, performance optimization, and market expansion, with a strong foundation in data analysis, reporting, and dashboard creation using tools like Excel, Power BI, and Power Query.

Throughout my career, I’ve led teams to exceed sales targets, optimized inventory and promotional strategies, and enhanced brand performance. My journey spans impactful roles such as Brand Manager, Agency Sales Manager, Deputy Sales Manager, Assistant Sales Development Manager & Marketing Executive in Myanmar’s leading organizations. I’ve driven measurable growth—like a 49% improvement in stock turnover, 18% revenue growth, and a 56% boost in target achievements—by blending strategic planning with actionable data insights.

Armed with an MBA and BBA in Business Management from Yangon University of Economics, I’m also equipped with practical skills in automation tools like Power Automate, CRM systems, and SQL. My analytical mindset is complemented by a passion for continuous improvement and technology adoption to enhance organizational efficiency and strategic planning.

Beyond work, I’m multilingual (fluent in English, Hindi, and Burmese), committed to leadership development, and actively contribute to community initiatives such as the India Education Fair. I bring with me not just experience, but a deep drive to deliver value through insights, strategy, and innovation.
