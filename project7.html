Project Overview

This project focuses on designing an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze pizza sales performance across various categories, sizes, and time periods. The goal is to uncover key business insights such as peak sales hours, best-selling pizzas, category-wise contributions, and weekly or monthly order trends to drive data-driven decision-making for a pizza restaurant chain.

The solution is designed for restaurant operations, marketing, and supply chain teams who rely on real-time sales insights to optimize inventory, promotions, and staffing.
